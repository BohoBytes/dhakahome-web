Frequently Asked Questions

How does Dhaka Homes manage residential properties?

We offer a hassle-free Residential Property Management service for owners living in Bangladesh or abroad. Our team handles everything on your behalf for a monthly service fee—saving you time, stress, and unnecessary back-and-forth.

Our services include:

  • Preparing your property for the rental market
  • Marketing through signage, social media, newspaper, and our client network
  • Conducting in-person tours for potential tenants
  • Shortlisting, screening, and verifying tenant backgrounds
  • Preparing and finalizing the Tenancy Agreement with all required clauses
  • Paying utility bills (DESCO, WASA, GAS) on your behalf
  • Ensuring tenants pay service charges on time
  • Preparing and sharing monthly statements of accounts and receipts
  • Conducting periodic inspections to ensure tenants maintain the property properly
  • Assisting with Municipal Tax and Land Khazna payments
  • Representing you in Owner Association meetings when needed
  • Collecting monthly rent and depositing it to your designated bank account

Key Holding & Supervision of Vacant Flat

Flat owners—local or NRB—who keep their property vacant but livable can rely on us for regular care. We make sure the home stays safe, secure, and well-maintained, giving you peace of mind even when you're away.

  • Pay your utility bills (DESCO, WASA, GAS) with the respective authorities or bank booths.
  • Participate in Owner Association meetings on behalf of the owner.
  • Ensure tenants pay all service charges on time.
  • Assist with Municipal Tax and Land Khazna payments.
  • Visit the property periodically to keep it in good condition.
  • Clean (dust-free) and air out the flat/apartment every month.

One Time Apartment Rental

Looking to rent out your property? We help you find the right tenants with the following services included in the first month's rent:

  • Preparing your property for the rental market
  • Marketing through signage, social media, newspaper, and our client network
  • Conducting in-person tours for prospective tenants
  • Shortlisting, screening, and verifying tenant profiles
  • Preparing and finalizing the Tenancy Agreement with all required clauses

How does Dhaka Homes manage commercial properties?

Hotel & Restaurant Management

We and our sister concerns manage hotels across Cox's Bazar and St. Martin's Island, along with six restaurants located within them.

  • Praasad Paradise (Cox's Bazar, three-star)
  • Cox Vacation Hotel
  • White Palace Hotel
  • Shakira Beach Resort
  • Praasad Paradise Hotel (St. Martin's Island)

From human resources to strategic marketing, we handle every aspect of managing large and small hotels and restaurants to generate profitable revenue for our clients. You're welcome to visit any of our hotels to experience our service and management style.

Commercial Building & Shops

We help you list and market commercial space for rent, negotiate and execute contracts on your behalf, and provide monthly services to manage payments and documentation.

  • Collect, deposit, and pay all dues including utilities and taxes
  • Manage small and large shops across Dhaka, including New Market City Complex and Mirpur City Center
  • Support both standalone shops and large commercial buildings

Whether you need a one-time listing or ongoing management, we tailor the service to your requirements.
